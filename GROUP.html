Group Leader

        Dr. Yunqian Dai is a Professor of Chemistry at Southast University (SEU) . Dr. Dai was born in Sichuan, China, in 1984. She received a B.S. degree in chemical engineering and technology in 2006, and a Ph.D. degree in material physics and chemistry under the tutelage of Prof. Yueming Sun and Prof. Younan Xia(from 2008 to 2010, in the cooperative training project at the University of Washington in Seattle) in 2011. Dr.Dai has published more than 20 papers in prestigious journals such as Science,J. Am. Chem. Soc., Angew. Chem. Int. Ed., Chem. Soc. Rev. and Adv.Mater.. As of April 2016, her scientific works have been cited more than 400 times.
